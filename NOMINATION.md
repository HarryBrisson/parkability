# Nomination provenance

This metric was **nominated by a resident of Ward 1** (Chicago). Per the
ward-wise-civic-tech / Penlight convention, resident-nominated metrics are
credited to the nominating ward and counted toward that ward's
`metrics_submitted_count` (the "all 50 wards" nomination goal).

When parkability is wired into Penlight, its metric metadata carries
`nominator.ward_id = "01"` so the nomination is counted for Ward 1.
